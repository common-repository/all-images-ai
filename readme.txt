== Description ==

Transform your WordPress site into a visual masterpiece with the All Images plugin. Whether you're a blogger, a theme developer, or a digital marketer, All Images brings the power of visual enhancement right within your WordPress dashboard. This powerful plugin not only improves the aesthetic appeal of your site but also boosts engagement and SEO performance through its unique features.

= Core Services =
1. **AI Image Bank + Free Stock Photo Image Bank:** Gain instant access to over 2 million+ unique images from our extensive library and popular free stock photo platforms like Unsplash, Pixabay, and Pexels. This service allows you to effortlessly add high-quality visuals to your website without leaving WordPress, ensuring your site always has fresh, captivating content.

2. **Custom Image Creation:** Tailor images to fit the essence of your content. This feature leverages AI to generate images based on specific needs, such as post titles, categories, or H2 tags. Each image is perfectly aligned with your content’s theme, enhancing the overall coherence and visual impact of your site.

3. **Automated Bulk Images:** Streamline the visual enhancement of your site with our AI that intelligently adds images to new posts, pages, or portfolios. This service automates the process of maintaining a visually appealing site by automatically populating it with relevant images, saving you time and effort.

**/!\ Exclusive Image Usage:** The best part about using All Images is the exclusivity it offers. Once you download any image, it is removed from our accessible database, and **you become the sole holder of that image**, ensuring that no one else can purchase or use the same image on their website. This unique feature sets All Images apart, providing you with exclusive content that truly stands out. Moreover, Google values unique content, and using exclusive images can significantly enhance your SEO, helping your site rank higher in search results and attract more organic traffic.
Transform your WordPress into a dynamic visual platform with All Images – where functionality meets creativity and exclusivity.

= Features =
– **Expansive Image Search**: Quickly navigate through multiple vast databases to find the right image for any topic within seconds.

– **Flexible Image Orientation**: Choose from landscape, portrait, or square formats to fit the design of your page perfectly.

– **Efficiency at Its Best**: Save time by uploading high-quality stock images directly from the WordPress admin panel, streamlining your workflow.

– **Developer-Friendly**: Ideal for theme and plugin developers looking to prototype and build with tailor-made imagery.

– **Enhanced Engagement for Marketers**: Boost your site’s engagement effortlessly, eliminating the need for costly graphic design resources.

– **Gutenberg Integration**: Works seamlessly within Gutenberg through a dedicated plugin sidebar, enhancing your editing experience.

– **Comprehensive Media Modal**: Accessible as a tab within the WordPress Media Modal, making it easy to manage visuals.
Page Builder Compatibility: Integrates smoothly with major page builders like Elementor, Beaver Builder, WP Bakery, Brizy, and Divi.

– **Metadata Management**: Fine-tune image metadata (filename, alt text, caption) before upload to maximize SEO impact.

– **Accessibility Features**: Automatically includes relevant alt descriptions for improved accessibility and SEO.

– **User-Friendly Interface**: Simple and intuitive—select an image, and it’s instantly uploaded to your media library.

Upgrade your WordPress site with All Images, and turn every post into a visual treat that captures and retains your audience’s attention.

3. Optional: configure providers' apis keys from the plugin settings page
